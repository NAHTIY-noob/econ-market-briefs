# Gold correction and safe-haven credibility

**Date:** 2026-03-29

## What happened
Gold fell sharply before dip-buyers stepped in.

## Why it matters
The move challenges the idea that gold always behaves as a reliable safe haven during geopolitical stress.

## Mechanism chain
Geopolitical shock → flight to dollar liquidity → stronger USD → pressure on gold in the short run

## What most people miss
Safe-haven demand is not one-dimensional. In panic episodes, investors may prefer dollar cash or Treasuries before rotating into gold.

## Market implication
Short-term gold weakness does not automatically invalidate longer-term inflation, debt, and reserve-diversification support.

## What to watch next
- USD index
- Real yields
- Central-bank buying
- ETF flows

## My view
The correction may be a positioning reset rather than a full narrative breakdown.
